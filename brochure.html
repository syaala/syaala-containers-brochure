How It Works

From order to operational in 8 weeks

Week 1-2: Design & Configuration

Work with our team to spec your exact requirements. Choose pod configurations, networking topology, and integration points. Receive detailed CAD layouts and power projections.

Week 3-6: Manufacturing

Pods are built, tested, and validated at our facilities. Every unit undergoes burn-in testing at full load. Hardware is installed, configured, and documented.

Week 7: Shipping & Site Prep

Containers ship globally via standard freight. While in transit, your site team prepares power feeds and network drops using our provided specifications.

Week 8: Deployment

Pods arrive on-site. Our field team connects power, cooling, and networking. Systems are validated and handed over production-ready.

From signed contract to operational infrastructure in 8 weeks. Traditional data centers take 24 months.
